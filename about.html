About Our Church

RCCG New Life Assembly Area is a vibrant community of belivers dedicated to spreading the gospel of Jesus Christ. We are part of the Redeemed Christian Church Of God, General Overseer: Pastor Enoch Adeboye

RCCG Mission & Vision:

  • To make Heaven.
  • To take as many people as possible with us.
  • To have a member of RCCG in every family of all nation.
  • To accomplish No.1 above, holiness will be our lifestyle.
  • To accomplish No.2 an 3 above we will plant churches with 5 minitues walking distance in every city and town of developing countries and within five minitues driving distance in every city and town of developed countries.
  • We will pursue these objectives until every Nation in the world is reached for the Lord Jesus Christ.
